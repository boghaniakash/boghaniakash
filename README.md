## 🚀 About Me

```swift

    role:      ["iOS Engineer", "Android Engineer", "Indie Founder"],
```

- 🍎🤖 I design, build, and ship **iOS & Android apps** solo — from first sketch to a live listing.
- 🏢 Founder of **Trinava Tech**, an independent app studio with a growing portfolio of utility, media, launcher & game apps.
- 📈 I don't just write code — I own the whole product loop: **ASO**, **AdMob monetization**, **Google Ads user acquisition**, and **Firebase / GA4 analytics**.
- 🎬 Currently shipping **MovieBox** and experimenting with new app ideas.
- 📊 Background in **algo-trading** and Zerodha Kite Connect integrations.
- 💡 Always curious about what makes an app *useful enough that people keep it*.
